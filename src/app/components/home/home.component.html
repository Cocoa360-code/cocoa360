Our MODEL

FARM-FOR-IMPACT

Our proprietary model integrates community engagement with self-sustainability, utilizing volunteer labor and climate smart farm management to generate funds. Each season, community participation yield profits directed through local decision-making to enhance health and education outcomes for the communities and their families.
