Childhood Education

Rural Education Outcomes

Our implementation of the model at Tarkwa Breman Girls' School shows a significant positive impact on rural education outcomes. Throughout the COVID-19 pandemic we maintained high attendance rates 98% with home visits and a public health education curriculum.

Community Health

Clinic Results

The Tarkwa Breman Community Clinic (TBCC) brings care to the cocoa farmers of Tarkwa Breman and its 7 other surrounding villages with outstanding health results. Our clinic is staffed with a physician assistant, a midwife, a nurse, a pharmacist, and a clerk. TBCC has bridged the majority of healthcare gaps, specifically infant and maternity care, in Tarkwa Breman.

Every year we serve 121% more patients.

Highlights

Established health clinic in 2016

1,000+ healthcare home visits every year

HIV testing, primary care, maternal care, & reproductive care services
